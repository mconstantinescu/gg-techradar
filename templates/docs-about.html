About Technical Documentation

The Documentation section is the third pillar of the Gunvor Group Technology Portal. While the Technology Radar answers "what technologies do we track?" and Decision Records capture "why did we choose them?", this section provides the "how" — detailed, actionable guidance for building, operating, and securing our technology landscape.

The Governance Flow

  Technologies (Radar)  →  Decisions (TDR/ADR)  →  Documentation (Docs)
        "What"                   "Why"                    "How"

Each document links back to the Radar blips and Decision Records that inform it, creating a traceable chain from technology assessment through decision rationale to implementation guidance.

Document Types

Documentation is organised by the type of content, not by technology domain. This ensures engineers can quickly find the right kind of guidance regardless of the technology involved.

Blueprints & Reference Architectures

Canonical architecture patterns and reference designs for common workloads — the "what does good look like" templates.

Golden Paths

Step-by-step guides for the recommended way to build, deploy, and operate services. Follow the paved road.

Standard Operating Procedures

Runbooks and operational procedures for day-to-day platform and service management — the "what to do when" guides.

Policies & Standards

Mandatory policies, compliance baselines, and security standards that all teams must follow.

Whitepapers & Guides

In-depth technical guides, best-practice whitepapers, and reference material for deep dives.

Relationship to Radar & Decisions

Section Answers Audience
Technology Radar "What technologies do we track and recommend?" All engineering staff
Decision Records "Why did we choose this technology or approach?" TAG, architects, all teams
Documentation "How do we implement, operate, and secure it?" Engineers, SRE, platform teams

Cross-Linking

Every document includes Related links to:

  • Decision Records — the TDR/ADR that mandates or recommends the approach
  • Radar blips — the technology entries that inform the document
  • Other docs — companion documentation in the same or related categories

Contributing

To add or update documentation:

  1. Create or edit a markdown file in the 2026-03-01/ edition folder of the Documents repository.
  2. Use the standard frontmatter format with the appropriate category (document type).
  3. Open a pull request — documentation changes require at least one reviewer.

Frontmatter Example

---
title: My New Golden Path
category: Golden Paths
tags: kubernetes, deployment, ci-cd
author: Technology Advisory Group
description: Step-by-step guide for deploying services to AKS.
related_decisions:
  - kubernetes-production
related_radar:
  - kubernetes
  - helm
flag: new
---

Valid categories: Blueprints & Reference Architectures, Golden Paths, Standard Operating Procedures, Policies & Standards, Whitepapers & Guides.
