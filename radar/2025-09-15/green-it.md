As digitalisation accelerates, the energy consumption of IT systems continues to grow. Green IT focuses on reducing the environmental impact of digital systems across architecture, development, and operations.

At Gunvor Group, we encourage sustainable practices such as lean and modular design (build only what is needed), energy-aware language and runtime choices, workload scheduling aligned with renewable-energy availability, avoidance of over-provisioning, and preference for cloud regions powered by renewable energy. Key metrics we track include operational energy consumption, embodied carbon, and CO₂ emissions per unit of work. Green IT has moved from an aspirational goal to an active engineering concern.
