The [TM Forum Open Digital Architecture (ODA)](https://www.tmforum.org/oda/) provides standards-based architectural blueprints built on modern patterns such as microservices, cloud-native design, and API-first principles. It offers a modular and flexible reference architecture that can accelerate delivery, simplify management, and enhance customer experience.

At Gunvor Group, we assess ODA concepts selectively where they align with our platform architecture — particularly the API lifecycle management and component-model aspects. While ODA originates in the telco domain, its principles around standardised interfaces and composable architecture are broadly applicable to enterprise IT.
