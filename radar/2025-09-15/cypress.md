[Cypress](https://www.cypress.io/) continues to be a key testing tool at Gunvor Group for component and end-to-end testing of web applications. Its developer-friendly API, time-travel debugging, and native TypeScript support make it highly productive for front-end teams.

Recent versions have broadened browser support (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, WebKit), improved execution speed, and strengthened CI/CD integrations with platforms like GitHub Actions and GitLab CI. For new projects that require cross-browser testing or multi-tab scenarios, we also evaluate [Playwright](/tools/playwright/) — but Cypress remains a proven choice for teams already invested in its ecosystem.
