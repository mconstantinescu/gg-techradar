Our world is increasingly complex. Societies and economic systems evolve rapidly. Uncertainty and unexpected developments are part of everyday reality — especially in cross-functional IT organisations working on digitalisation, transformation, and innovation.

**Complexity management capabilities** describe how well an individual or team can handle conditions where cause and effect are not clearly linked and decision-making cannot be structured linearly. The more perspectives considered, the more differentiated the analysis, and the faster this is done — the better complexity is managed.

At Gunvor Group, we encourage teams to invest in systemic thinking, reflective practices, and structured collaboration techniques such as [Event Storming](/methods-and-patterns/event-storming/) and [Collaborative Modeling](/methods-and-patterns/collaborative-modeling/). Understanding communication dynamics — and which conditions enable effective interaction — helps us address complexity constructively as a source of creativity rather than a barrier.
