[Adobe Creative Cloud](https://www.adobe.com/creativecloud.html) provides a comprehensive creative platform that we use across design and UX workflows. Applications such as Adobe XD, Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign each serve a distinct role in our design process — from wireframing and prototyping to asset production and print-ready layouts.

The integration between applications and Creative Cloud Libraries enables efficient asset sharing across teams. We use Figma for collaborative UI design, but Adobe Creative Cloud remains the standard for photo editing, illustration, motion graphics, and publishing at Gunvor Group.
