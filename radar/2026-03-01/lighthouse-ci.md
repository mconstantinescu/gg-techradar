[Lighthouse CI](https://github.com/GoogleChrome/lighthouse-ci) integrates Google Lighthouse audits into our CI/CD pipelines, providing continuous monitoring of performance, accessibility, SEO, and best-practice compliance for web applications.

At Gunvor Group, we use Lighthouse CI to set performance budgets and detect regressions before they reach production. Historical trend data enables teams to track improvements over time and make data-driven decisions about front-end optimisation priorities.
