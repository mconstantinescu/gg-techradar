[Event Storming](https://www.eventstorming.com/) is a collaborative workshop method for exploring and modelling complex business domains through domain events. Rather than starting with abstract requirements, it begins with concrete events and works backward to identify triggers, actors, and responsibilities.

At Gunvor Group, Event Storming is a core practice for domain discovery in new projects and for refining bounded contexts in existing systems. It supports Domain-Driven Design principles, helps establish a ubiquitous language, and produces artefacts that directly inform service boundaries and architectural decisions — especially in event-driven systems using patterns such as CQRS and event sourcing.
