[Effect](https://effect.website/) is a TypeScript library for building type-safe, composable, and concurrent applications. It provides a structured approach to error handling, dependency injection, concurrency, and resource management — concepts familiar from languages like Scala (ZIO) and Haskell.

At Gunvor Group, we are assessing Effect for backend TypeScript services where its structured concurrency, typed error channels, and retry/timeout primitives could replace ad-hoc patterns. The learning curve is significant, but the type-safety guarantees and compositional model are compelling for complex business logic.
