Platform engineering is the discipline of designing and building internal developer platforms (IDPs) that abstract infrastructure complexity and provide self-service capabilities to product teams. Rather than each team managing its own infrastructure, a dedicated platform team provides golden paths for common workloads.

At Gunvor Group, our platform engineering practice centres on [Backstage](/tools/backstage/) as the developer portal, [Kubernetes](/platforms-and-services/kubernetes/) as the compute layer, [GitOps](/methods-and-patterns/gitops/) for deployment, and Terraform/OpenTofu for infrastructure provisioning. The platform team provides reusable templates, CI/CD pipelines, and observability tooling — enabling product teams to ship faster with consistent quality and security guardrails.
